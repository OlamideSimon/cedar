About Us

Learn more about us

Transparency and Accountability in Totality is a non-governmental organization that supports the role of non-state and state actors in promoting and strengthening institutions to become more proactive, responsive, and accountable. We strengthen citizens' and marginalized groups' capacity to participate in democratic governance processes by simplifying and translating government data into local dialects for the citizens to hold the government accountable for their actions and inaction.

Areas of focus and

01NATURAL RESOURCE GOVERNANCE (Beneficial Ownership, Community Development Agreement CDA)

We focus on promoting Natural Resource Governance and emphasize Beneficial Ownership and Community Development Agreements (CDAs). We aim to enhance transparency and accountability in the extractive industries, monitor mining companies' compliance with taxation, royalty payments, and CDAs, ensure adherence to policies while evaluating mining impacts, and advocate for adequate compensation for affected communities.

02DOMESTIC RESOURCE MOBILIZATION, CLIMATE FINANCING AND FISCAL PLANNING GOVERNANCE (Taxation, climate financing, Budget, Audit, Procurement and Debt Management);

Our focus on Domestic Resource Mobilization, Climate Financing, and Fiscal Planning Governance encompasses taxation, budget, audit, procurement, and debt management in simplify taxation processes at national and subnational levels, support voluntary compliance, and enhance tax inclusivity, especially in the informal sector, advocate for allocating a portion of taxes from climate-impacting companies to climate financing, ensuring transparency and accountability. ensuring compliance and community ownership. We will analyze budgets to assess implementation and performance, disseminate findings for public accountability, and monitor audited reports for financial management irregularities.

03OTHER INITIATIVES (Caregiver, My-school project, Shin-kun-sheda community engagement)

In our effort to promote a fairer distribution of caregiving responsibilities, empower women, and enhance the recognition and value of care work within the society. The organization has keyed into the Caregiver initiative to alleviate gender disparities and obstacles women and care workers face, particularly in unpaid and underpaid care roles. The aim is to witness an increased recognition and value placed on the crucial contributions of caregivers while acknowledging their pivotal role in sustaining the overall well-being of the community.

Furthermore, the organization will be strengthening the capacity of its staff and volunteers to expand the myschool project initiative. The organization will map more schools in Kaduna, Bauchi, and any other State that will be identified and approved by the Board of Management. The Shin-kun-sheda community engagement initiative will leverage our familiarity and knowledge of the communities across all states of implementation to physically amplify our projects in local dialects, which will be aired as a podcast across all social media handles.

Objectives

To improve transparency and accountability in the natural resource sector, focusing on beneficial ownership and community development agreements.

To advocate for policy reforms regarding royalty and tax payments to enhance revenue generation from the solid mineral sector.

To simplify taxation procedures to promote voluntary compliance, particularly among informal sector businesses, and address gender disparities in the tax system to enhance the domestic resource mobilization

To incorporate climate financing initiatives into resource mobilization efforts by advocating for a portion of taxes from companies contributing to climate change to be allocated towards climate financing.

To ensure compliance in the public procurement and budgeting processes to enhance transparency, and accountability through continuous monitoring, stakeholder engagement promoting community ownership of projects, and facilitating reporting of discrepancies.

Expand the My School Today project to map out schools, ensure accessibility, and address challenges faced by students in accessing educational facilities.

Establish Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) clinics in schools to promote climate action and awareness among students.
